Organic Shapes Study #1

Michael Dunn

This is a study of organic shapes created from a series of ten lines. The algorithm used to create the study was ten lines that start from the bottom left of the page and end in the top right.



Organic Shapes Study #2

Michael Dunn

This is a second study of organic shapes. The algorithm used was based on using single lines to create organic shapes of varying complexities and contrasts. It was inspired by Chuck Close's pixelated portraits.



Organic Shapes Lantern

Michael Dunn

This is a foldable lantern created from a series of organic shapes and conceived from the organic shapes studies. The design is inspired by living hinges and was created to be cut out of stiff materials such as cardboard and acrylic.
